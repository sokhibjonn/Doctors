Our Mission

Our Mission Our Team Careers
Empowering you to live your healthiest life through comprehensive,
personalised care.
We invite you to discover an experience that places you—the patient—at the centre. From our easy-to-navigate online booking system to car parking right outside the front door, we’ve made things fuss-free. With all your primary healthcare and general practice services in one place, we help you get the care you need as seamlessly as possible.

Our Burwood-based practice is a family-friendly environment, thoughtfully designed to promote a sense of calm. This safe and welcoming atmosphere extends to our judgement-free treatment rooms, where we want you to feel comfortable talking with a supportive GP. Our doctors have the latest medical knowledge and take the time to talk you through your options. We want you to feel empowered with the information you need to make the best healthcare decisions for you.

We’re here when you’re sick – and also when you’re not. With our proactive and preventative approach to physical and mental health, you can form a treatment plan and goals with your GP, to help motivate you to get healthier and live longer. We believe that’s the way healthcare should be.

Progressive

We’re committed to learning, growing and developing our team to deliver you exceptional, forward-thinking service and comprehensive preventative health care.




Empowering

Our wide range of on-site medical and allied health services empower you with the person-centred support, care and expertise you need to live your healthiest life.
















Our Team

While our Burwood clinic is new to the area, our expert team hails from a successful practice in bayside Melbourne. We’re proud to bring our learnings and experience from Bluff Road Medical to offer the same quality service in a new innovative health hub. We want you to experience a shared, collaborative patient journey. We’re committed to working as a team of doctors and allied health practitioners in partnership with each other – and with you.

Meet our team

Dr Peter Cheng

General Practitioner
BPharm, MBBS (Hons), FRACGP
Interests
Skin Cancer and Surgery,
Dermatology and Paediatrics

Peter completed his Medical Degree with Honours from Monash University in 2013. He spent many years honing his surgical acumen as a surgical trainee at the Royal Melbourne Hospital prior to transitioning to work as a GP and an Accredited Skin Doctor endorsed by the Skin Cancer College Australasia.

Peter is passionate about patient advocacy. His clinical journey, especially in his work prior as a pharmacist, has allowed him to understand that for most people, any form of medical visit can be daunting and anxiety-provoking. He has seen it from both sides of a patient’s medical journey. He eliminates all the medical jargon, and strives to make the experience smooth, and as pleasant as possible. He takes privilege in looking after those who trust him with their health and is very proud of the medicine he practices. He carries a motto of attaining the best possible outcome for his patients.

Peter’s clinical expertise extends to a wide array of medical and dermatological conditions. As a practitioner, his focus and goals are attaining holistic, pragmatic solutions to optimise long-term patient outcomes. He loves all facets of Family Medicine with special interests in Chronic Disease Management, Children’s Health, Dermatology, and Skin Cancer Medicine.

He is able to provide advice and treatments on a multiple array of skin conditions – including expert management on skin cancers – both Melanoma and Non – Melanoma Skin cancers.

This extends itself to simple non-surgical treatments, straightforward excisions to a wide array of more complex surgical work including flaps and grafts.

Peter continues to keep up-to-date, and strives to consistently improve learning the latest techniques and adopting the most up-to-date research to achieve the best care for his patients. He is currently on the pathway to achieving Fellowship with the Skin Cancer College of Australasia.

When not working, Peter is a “watch geek” at heart. He enjoys traveling, street photography, tennis, and spending time with his young family at home.




Dr Joan San

General Practitioner
MBBS, FRACGP, DRANZCOG
Interests
Travel Medicine, Women’s Health, Paediatrics, Skin Cancer and Minor Surgery

Joan graduated from the University of Melbourne in 1999 and undertook her residency years at St Vincent’s Hospital Melbourne. She then moved to Northeast Victoria to obtain her General Practice Registration (Rural Pathway) and Diploma in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. During that time, she worked as a rural GP obstetrician and provided services to the nearby ski medical centre, local district hospital and general practice.

She returned to Melbourne and joined the team at Bluff Road in 2007. Since then, she has completed an Expert Certificate in Dermoscopy (skin cancer diagnosis) and currently works regularly as a surgical assistant at Cabrini Hospital.

Joan enjoys the breadth and diversity of patients and challenges encountered in primary healthcare as well as educating and empowering oneself to respect their own health and well-being. She wishes she could travel and ski more but for now, enjoys spending time with her family and friends, the Arts and boxing.



Dr Jason Rajakulendran

General Practitioner & Travel Doctor
MBBS, MPH, FRACGP, DCH, CTH©
Interests
Paediatrics, men’s health, travel medicine, psychological and preventative health

Jason has a medical degree from Monash University and gained his hospital experience at Monash Health, before moving into General Practice. He worked in Albury/Wodonga and in the Northern Territory before returning to Melbourne as a practice principal. Jason has been a Registrar Alumni Coordinator and has spent many years as a GP Clinical Training Supervisor. He also has a Diploma of Child Health from Sydney University and a Masters of Public Health (International Health) from Monash University.

A keen traveller, Jason is a certified member of the International Society of Travel Medicine (ISTM), Australasian College of Tropical Medicine and part of the national Travel Medicine Alliance (TMA) network. He coordinates travel medicine at both Elevate Medical and Bluff Road Medical.

Jason has a keen interest in global travel, spending time with friends and family, AFL (Hawks, if you’re asking!), cricket, rugby union, films and music









Careers
